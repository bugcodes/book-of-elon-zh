# NOTES ON THIS BOOK
# 关于本书

---

**English:**
This is a book of Elon's ideas in his own words. I built this book by collecting his public statements, interviews, and writings over two decades. The goal is to present his thinking as clearly and directly as possible.

**中文：**
这是一本收录埃隆想法的书，全部使用他自己的话语。我通过收集他二十多年来的公开声明、采访和文章来构建这本书。目标是尽可能清晰、直接地呈现他的思想。

---

**English:**
All quotes are attributed to their sources in the back of the book. Where I've edited for clarity or brevity, I've indicated this with ellipses (...) or brackets [like this].

**中文：**
所有引用都在书后注明了来源。在为了清晰或简洁而编辑的地方，我用省略号（...）或括号 [像这样] 标明。

---

**English:**
This book is organized thematically rather than chronologically. The ideas are grouped by topic to make them more useful and actionable.

**中文：**
本书按主题组织，而非按时间顺序。这些想法按主题分组，使其更有用、更具可操作性。
